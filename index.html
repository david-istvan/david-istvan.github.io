Research

Inconsistency management in collaborative modeling settings

I am currently a member of the MSDL (Modelling, Simulation and Design Lab), working on my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Hans Vangheluwe and within the MBSE4Mechatronics project. My research focuses on inconsistency management in collaborative, multi-model system development and includes topics such as

  • optimizing engineering processes for consistency,
  • fundamental techniques for managing inconsistencies,
  • tolerance of inconsistencies, and
  • employing all these in tool integration scenarios.

Complex event processing (CEP) and its applications

VIATRA (VIsual Automated model TRAnsformations) is an event-driven and reactive model transformation platform. My primary contribution covers the complex event processing (CEP) module and the incremental API of the core transformation framework. This work is carried out in collaboration with the Fault Tolerant Systems Research Group of the Budapest University of Technology and Economics.
